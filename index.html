Our Story

ShorePointIT was founded with a clear mission: give growing organizations access to the same enterprise-grade IT strategy, cybersecurity, and cloud services that Fortune 500 companies rely on — with the agility and attention of a dedicated partner.

With over 20 years of enterprise IT leadership experience — including 18 years at Microsoft leading technology initiatives — our founder built ShorePointIT to bridge the gap between world-class technology expertise and the practical needs of organizations ready to modernize and scale.

Today, ShorePointIT serves organizations across healthcare, financial services, government, retail, and beyond — providing IT strategy, cloud transformation, cybersecurity, managed services, and executive-level technology consulting. We bring discipline, integrity, and a relentless focus on outcomes to every engagement.
