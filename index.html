Our Story

ShorePointIT was founded with a simple mission: give growing businesses access to the same enterprise-grade IT infrastructure, cybersecurity, and cloud services that Fortune 500 companies rely on — without the Fortune 500 price tag.

Based in New Jersey, we've built our reputation on responsiveness, technical excellence, and genuine partnership. We don't just fix things when they break — we architect secure, scalable technology environments that prevent problems before they start.

Today, ShorePointIT serves businesses across New Jersey and beyond, providing fully managed IT services, cybersecurity, cloud infrastructure, AI integration, and strategic technology consulting. We're proud to be a local team delivering world-class technology outcomes.
